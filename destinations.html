Destinations

  1. Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) – A Sacred Landmark

    2. An important Buddhist site in the world, the Temple of the Tooth Relic is located in the heart of Kandy. The sacred tooth of Buddha is kept in the temple, which is said to possess great spiritual significance.

  2. Mirissa

    3. Mirissa is a small beach town in Sri Lanka’s southern coast, which is becoming popular among beach lovers. Pristine golden sand beaches are perfect for sunbathing, calm waters perfect for snorkeling, surfing and whale watching.

  3. Dambulla Cave Temple

    4. Sri Lanka’s most impressive, sacred landmark is the Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site. The temple complex is a series of five caves, which have been carved out of a huge rock face each with beautiful murals and statues of Buddha. The temple itself is 1st century BCE and is still a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
